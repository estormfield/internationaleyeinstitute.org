Hospitalito Atitlan provides excellent medical care to the indigenous communities surrounding Lago Atitlan, Guatemala. This is the hospital where the volunteers of the International Eye Institute hold our eye clinics and perform eye surgeries for the people of the region. We are delighted to see, in this short video, some of the people IEI works with and we are happy to support their work. And we look forward to returning to Guatemala and to work with the wonderful people of Santiago Atitlan!

Wishing You Health & Happiness!

Your generosity has made it possible for the Hospitalito to continue to care for residents of Santiago Atitlán and environs despite the pandemic. We couldn't have done it without you! This video tells the story of Hospitalito Atitlán in the time of coronavirus.
