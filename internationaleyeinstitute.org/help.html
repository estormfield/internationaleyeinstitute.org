Contributions to the International Eye Institute

The International Eye Institute (IEI) welcomes your financial and material support for our work. The success of IEI is dependent upon securing monetary, in-kind and medical supply donations. We truly appreciate the support and encouragement you give us and we promise to be good stewards of your donations. Because of the willingness of our volunteers to spend their time, talents and money on this important work, we are able to leverage your donation dollars to an incredible extent.

IEI is a 501(c)(3) non-profit and your donations may be tax deductible to the full extent of the law. You can make donations by check.
